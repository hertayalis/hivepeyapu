Im a Pants Snob, and This Butt-Flattering Spanx Pair Is the Best

Publish date: 2024-07-19

You know those winter days when you wake up, start your pot of coffee, take a shower, do your makeup, and then go to your closet and…simply don’t know what to do. I just had that exact experience today — and it’s one I tend to every year post holidays, because I basically forget how to get dressed after living in sweats for two weeks straight (ahh, the magic of being home during Christmas). But alas, it’s time to get back to “real life,” which also means putting on “real pants,” though I refuse to sacrifice comfort for the sake of wearing bottoms. 

Good thing I don’t have to, which is why when I froze in front of my closet this morning; I said, “No Eva, don’t stress. You know exactly what to do,” and that’s to reach for my trusty Perfect Pants from Spanx that I wear more than any other piece of clothing I own (no joke). That’s because they wear like the butteriest, comfiest sweatpants you’ve ever put on but look like $400 designer trousers that you’d find hanging on the rack of Bergdorf Goodman. Chic? Yes. Easy to wear? Absolutely! Affordable? They’re sure as heck are right now.

My holy grail pants from Spanx, which are actually perfect because they are, well, perfect, are the cheapest I’ve ever seen them thanks to the brand’s end-of-season sale that has savings up to 70 percent. No, that’s not a typo, even though I thought there was a typo when I saw that my go-to $168 pants were currently $84.

Best Spanx Pants on Sale

The perfect wide-leg pants are the ideal balance of style and comfort, and you don’t find that often in good, reasonably priced bottoms. Add in the expensive-looking, four-way stretch ponte fabric that’s super soft and ultra-flattering, and you really have all the makings of perfect pants. My mom, sister, and I are all obsessed with them, and before you even ask, yes, we do own multiples, and yes, I will be stocking up on more while they’re the cheapest I’ve ever seen. 

My go-to pants aren’t the only ones marked down during Spanx's end-of-season sale. Many styles from the brand’s famous perfect collection are included, like the cropped kick flare that’s ideal for those who like a little bit of pizazz with their pants or an updated wide-leg silhouette with a split hem for even more movement.

All I can say is that everything I have purchased from Spanx has been among my most-worn pieces of all time, though nothing has received as much as my perfect wide-leg pants (except my fiancé, of course).
