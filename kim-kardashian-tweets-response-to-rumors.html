Kim Kardashian Speaks Out Following Khloe Pregnancy News

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Kim Kardashian responded to rumors about her family on Twitter this morning.

"Let me just say this...," the reality star tweeted. "People who supposedly work with us 'confirming' details they know nothing about! Especially when we havent [sic] even communicated with them SMH"

Her tweets arrive the morning after word broke that her sister Khloé Kardashian is reportedly expecting a child with her boyfriend Tristan Thompson. People confirmed the news. Kim's response, as vague as it may be, marks the first time anyone in the Kardashian-Jenner family has addressed Khloé's baby news.

Kim also responded to rumors that her half sister Kylie Jenner is also with child, which was first reported on Friday night. Kardashian retweeted a report on her reaction to Kylie’s pregnancy, calling it “a very fake story.”

She then addressed reports of Caitlyn Jenner’s response to the pregnancy news, saying the media outlets published “fake” quotes from Jenner.

"And speaking of fake stories... The media is super shady for posting fake quotes from Caitlyn when she hasn’t spoke to anyone," she said.
